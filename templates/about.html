About NutriEase

NutriEase is a self-hosted fitness & nutrition assistant designed to feel like talking to a friendly doctor — not a search bar. Ask in plain English, get clear answers and an actionable plan.

Built by Shivam Kumar

Hi, I'm Shivam Kumar. I built NutriEase to give people direct, jargon-free guidance on nutrition and fitness without selling them anything.

Email: codexshivam@gmail.com

Our principles

  • Food first, supplements second.
  • Plain language — no jargon walls.
  • Privacy by default — your data stays on your server.
  • Education, not diagnosis. We always recommend a real clinician for medical issues.

What you can do

  • Chat with a doctor-style coach trained on a large nutrition dataset.
  • Get a 7-day diet plan tailored to your goal and dietary preference.
  • Calculate BMI, BMR and daily calories with the Mifflin-St Jeor equation.
  • Track weight progress over time and save plan history.
  • Export any plan as a polished PDF.
